UAE History

The seven emirates were united in 1971 under his highness Sheikh Zayed Bin Sultan Al Nahyan. The seven rulers of the emirates selected Abu Dhabi to be the capital of UAE. Since its unification UAE has grown strongly in all areas of civil development.

The Seven Emirates in UAE are:

  • Abu Dhabi
    1. Abu Dhabi
    2. Al Ain
    3. Alsamhah
  • Dubai
  • Sharjah
  • Ajman
  • Om Alquwain
  • Al Fujairah
  • Ras Alkhaimah
